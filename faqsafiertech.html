What is AMC?

AMC stands for 'annual maintenance contract'.It is the service provided by an amc company in which company takes the responsiblity of maintenance of an equipment for an year time .

What is the cost an AMC for an year?

The cost of AMC contract depends on following factors.

  1. Type of equipment(i.e electrical or mechanical)
  2. Type of services(i.e with parts or without parts)
  3. Time period (i.e seasonal or annualy)

What is without parts AMC service?

Without parts amc includes the reparement of parts whereas if any part gets destroyed and can't be fixed instead requires a new part in that case, the amc company is not liable for the purchase of the new part to replace the faulty one. The part needs to provided by the client company itself .

What is with part AMC service?

With part amc includes the reparement of parts as well as if any part get destroyed and can't be fixed then amc company is responsible of the purchase of part and fixing the part whereas client company need not to provide the parts. The sole responsiblity of change and service of part is completely on amc company.

What type of amc services are provided by safiertech?

Types of amc services provided by safiertech pvt. ltd.

  1. seasonal and annual
  2. with parts and without parts
  3. electrical and mechanical
